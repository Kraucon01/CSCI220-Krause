Introducing Warhammer 40k



Info About the Hobby

It is the 41st Millennium. For more than a hundred centuries The Emperor has sat immobile on the Golden Throne of Earth.
He is the Master of Mankind by the will of the gods, and master of a million worlds by the might of his inexhaustible armies.
He is a rotting carcass writhing invisibly with power from the Dark Age of Technology. He is the Carrion Lord of the Imperium
for whom a thousand souls are sacrificed every day, so that he may never truly die.
Yet even in his deathless state, the Emperor continues his eternal vigilance. Mighty battlefleets cross the daemon-infested
miasma of the Warp, the only route between distant stars, their way lit by the Astronomican, the psychic manifestation of
the Emperor's will. Vast armies give battle in his name on uncounted worlds. Greatest amongst his soldiers are the
Adeptus Astartes, the Space Marines, bio-engineered super-warriors. Their comrades in arms are legion: the Imperial Guard
and countless planetary defence forces, the ever vigilant Inquisition and the tech-priests of the Adeptus Mechanicus to
name only a few. But for all their multitudes, they are barely enough to hold off the ever-present threat from aliens,
heretics, mutants - and worse.
To be a man in such times is to be one amongst untold billions. It is to live in the cruelest and most bloody
regime imaginable. These are the tales of those times. Forget the power of technology and science, for so much
has been forgotten, never to be re-learned. Forget the promise of progress and understanding, for in the grim dark
future there is only war. There is no peace amongst the stars, only an eternity of carnage and slaughter, and the
laughter of thirsting gods.

If you couldn't tell the setting for this particular hobby is a very dark and depressing place with forms of comedy
sprinkled in here and there. The main hobby itself is about collecting paintable miniatures from one, or more, factions
within the setting. Now you can just collect the models and not worry about painting them if you just want to use them
in a game for the hobby.The inverse is true as well and trust me when I say there are some really beautiful and amazing
painted models out there. It also includes tournaments, both local and official, that you can enter if your feeling
competitive.

However, since the purpose of this video is to introduce new comers to the hobby this site won't go to deep into the lore.
But, there will be links down at the bottom of this page for you to check out if you are interested in learning about the lore.

The Imperium of Mankind

The Imperium of Man is the galaxy spanning empire of mankind that has managed to sustain itself even after the fall of their
now skeletal Emperor. They are considered one of the big bads in the universe do to the simple fact that man doesn't care for
the lives of its people if they do not serve their Emperor. They are gothic by design and, quite frankly, possesses the most
subfactions with in themselves. Such as the infamous poster boys of the Space Marines and all of their own seperate factions.
Along with the Adeptus Custodes, Soroitas, the Inquisition, the Astra Militarum, and many more. If you notices the pattern here
then you realize that man has become a religious parody of itself that will kill each other left and right if they suspect you
their supposed *ahem* HERESY. But, they are the most basic yet versitaile force in their galaxy, well so
long as they can stop killing each other in the millions.

The Xeno's from the Stars

The Xenos is the generic term given to just about all non-human races within the universe that range from mystical space elves
all the way to giant planet eating bugs. Now, like the Imperium they have a quite a few sub factions under this general title
but they are all much more specialized in a certain style of combat that while overlaps a little is still unique to each faction.
This can range from being a horde of glass cannons that you need to play weirdly to win, letting lady luck take the wheel while
going ham, and even just being the ever paitent firing line that will shoot anyone that attacks their units. However, due to
these specializations they can be hard countered at times if they don't prepare for them in advance. Additionally, While some
factions can work together in an army they need to be of the same race and they need to have some form of synergy. However, if
you really want a challenge and love doing a specific thing really well then any of the Xenos races are for you. Also as a bit
of warning to all be prepared to be called all kinds of things depending on the which of the races you play. These are some but
but not limited to edgy sweat goblin, greenskin rng boiz, pansies, space weeb communist and many more given the fanbases need to
reuse some old jokes for the sake of fun. This is all only in jest so if you find something cool then just play it.


The Daemons of Chaos


This faction is all about the supernatural forces of the universe that stems from this place called "The Warp". Now this place
is sort of like the inside of a black hole mixed with the collect thoughts, feelings, and emotions of all soul bearing species.
Combine it with the fact that it is ruled by four of the most terrifying and down right malicious monsters that exist and you
basically have what many in the community dub as Super Ultra Murder Death Hell. Now, the sub factions in this one
are a bit more off the wall considering they actually allow you to just have a hoard of Daemons ready to go and murder just
anything they can. They also have their own Space Marines that are called Chaos Marines that betrayed their race to
to serve one of the main driving forces in the Warp known only as The Chaos Gods. There are four in total, Tzeench, Khorne, Nergal
and Slaneesh. Additionally, Daemons are just smaller aspects of these gods that can be brought back at any time if they are
destroyed. This combined with the fact that just knowing Chaos exists can turn anyone into their slaves makes this particular
faction seem like it is just an unstoppable force that will never end. In all honesty, lore wise this is exactly what they are
but on the table top they are mearly a go in and hit face style of army when they don't have the Chaos Marines and just purly
Daemons. If you wish to know about these supposed gods there is a link just below in the recources that can explain what they are
and why they are considered gods and malevolent beings.
