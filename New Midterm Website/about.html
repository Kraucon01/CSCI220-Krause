About This Website

This website's purpose is to act an an introductory to the hobby know as Warhammer 40k and the factions you can choose from.
While it won't go to deep into everything about the hobby it will act as a way to get you the viewer interested in it. This
hobby is a very niche thing considering the setting and current story of it but it is very enjoyable for many people. There
are also links in the home page of the site that will allow you to sample the hobby and community in an enjoyable and well
paced fashion. This way you as the viewer/outsider of this hobby can actually see what it is about and if it isn't for you
then you will have seen it but not spent anything on it. Now then, with this little bit out of the way hopefully you enjoy
everything in this website and hope you have a good time. If not, it is completely understandable.
